## Purpose
Document key decisions made by the Business Analyst during the project
to ensure transparency and accountability.

## Decision Categories
- Scope clarification
- Requirement prioritization
- Process standardization vs customization
- Data ownership and governance
- Change request impact assessment

## Sample Decision Structure

| Decision Area | Description |
|--------------|------------|
| Context | Business or technical situation |
| Options | Available alternatives |
| Decision | Selected option |
| Rationale | Business & delivery justification |
| Impact | Scope / Cost / Timeline |
| Stakeholders | Involved parties |

## BA Authority Boundary
- BA proposes and analyzes
- Business approves
- Project governance enforces

## Managerial Value
- Demonstrates analytical thinking
- Shows risk awareness
- Builds trust in BA judgment
